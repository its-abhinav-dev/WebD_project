SUJATA ACHREKAR BIOGRAPHY

Sujata Achrekar is a renowned Indian artist celebrated for her vivid depictions of Indian culture and spirituality. Her work beautifully blends traditional motifs with contemporary aesthetics, creating a harmonious balance between the past and the present.



Born and raised in Mumbai, Sujata found her inspiration in the bustling streets, temples, and diverse traditions of India. Her "Ganesha Series" and abstract sculptures have gained international acclaim for their intricate detailing and profound symbolism.


Sujata has showcased her artwork in numerous exhibitions across India and internationally, with notable displays in London, Singapore, and New York. Her work continues to inspire art enthusiasts around the globe.
